Google Calendar
for Tourism

Scheduling, coordination, and never missing a booking

Calendar Uses

How tourism pros use Calendar

📅 Tour Schedule

Block dates for confirmed tours with guest count, package details

👥 Team Coordination

Assign guides, drivers to specific tours; everyone sees the schedule

⏰ Reminders

Auto-remind: "Send pre-trip email tomorrow" or "Confirm transport"

🚫 Availability

Block full dates to prevent overbooking; show "Sold Out"

📍 Location Info

Add meeting point address – opens in Google Maps directly

🔗 Attachments

Attach itinerary PDF or guest manifest to the calendar event

Activity 2

Create a calendar plan

📝 Task: Create a tour coordination calendar plan for the following week:

Scenario: You have 3 confirmed tours next week:
• Monday: Island Hopping – Garcia family (5 pax), 7 AM-4 PM, Hilton Port pickup
• Wednesday: City Heritage Tour – Kim group (8 pax), 9 AM-12 PM, Magellan's Cross
• Saturday: Whale Shark + Kawasan – Lee couple (2 pax), 4 AM-6 PM, hotel pickup

Create in Google Calendar:
• 3 tour events with complete details (title, time, location, description)
• 3 reminders: "Send pre-trip email" – 2 days before each tour
• 1 team meeting: "Weekly tour briefing" – Sunday 4 PM, 30 min

⏱️ 15 minutes • Use color-coding for different tour types

Pro Tips

Email + Calendar integration

  • Gmail → Calendar: Booking confirmations with dates auto-appear in Calendar
  • Calendar → Email: Send meeting invites directly from Calendar (guests get email)
  • Shared calendars: Create a "Tours" calendar shared with entire team
  • Multiple calendars: Personal + Tours + Meetings – toggle visibility
  • Mobile sync: Calendar on phone = always know your schedule on-the-go
