Concepts

Key booking terms

📋 Reservation

A held slot/room/seat pending confirmation or payment

✅ Confirmation

Official acceptance of booking, usually after payment

🎫 Voucher

Document proving the booking, presented upon arrival

❌ Cancellation

Guest or business cancels; policy determines refund

🔄 Modification

Changing dates, pax, or details of existing booking

⏰ No-show

Guest doesn't arrive without cancelling; policy applies

GDS & Property Management
System Overview

Understanding core tourism technology systems

Joshua E. Nisnisan
GDS

Global Distribution Systems

  • What: Network connecting airlines, hotels, car rentals to travel agents worldwide
  • Major players: Amadeus, Sabre, Travelport (Galileo/Worldspan)
  • Purpose: Real-time inventory, pricing, booking across thousands of suppliers
  • Users: Travel agencies, corporate travel managers, OTAs (backend)
  • Tourism role: How your flight/hotel gets booked through any agency worldwide
PMS

Property Management Systems

🛏️ Room Management

Room inventory, types, rates, availability calendar, housekeeping status

👤 Guest Records

Check-in/out, guest profiles, preferences, history, loyalty

💰 Billing

Guest folio, charges, payments, invoices, night audit

📊 Reporting

Occupancy rates, revenue, forecasting, performance

🔗 Integrations

Channel manager, POS, accounting, housekeeping apps

📱 Examples

Opera (Oracle), Cloudbeds, RoomRaccoon, eZee

CRM Concept

What is CRM?

  • Definition: Systematic approach to managing customer interactions and data
  • Purpose: Better service, repeat bookings, personalization, loyalty
  • Data tracked: Contact info, preferences, booking history, communication log, feedback
  • Simple version: A well-organized Google Sheet can BE your CRM
  • Advanced: HubSpot, Salesforce, Zoho CRM (for larger operations)

💡 Tourism example: "Welcome back, Ms. Garcia! Last time you requested extra pillows and the ocean-view room. Shall we arrange the same?" – That's CRM in action.

Payment Systems

How money moves in tourism

💳 POS

Point-of-sale: terminals at restaurants, shops, front desks for card/cash transactions

📱 Mobile Wallets

GCash, Maya: dominant in PH tourism for deposits and full payments

🌐 Online Gateways

PayPal, Stripe, DragonPay: for website checkout and international payments

🏦 Bank Transfer

BDO, BPI, UnionBank: for corporate bookings and large amounts

🧾 E-receipts

Digital proof of payment, important for record-keeping and disputes

🔄 Reconciliation

Matching payments received with bookings confirmed – daily essential
