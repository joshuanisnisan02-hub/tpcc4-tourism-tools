Fundamentals

Tourism marketing framework

👥 Audience

WHO are you targeting? Families? Couples? Backpackers? Age, budget, interests, travel style

💎 Value Proposition

WHY should they choose YOU? What's unique? "Only operator with sunset cruise included"

📣 Channels

WHERE will you reach them? Facebook? Instagram? TikTok? Google? Email? All?

🎯 Content Goals

WHAT do you want them to DO? Inquire? Book? Share? Follow? Visit your page?

💬 Message

HOW do you communicate? Tone, style, language. Fun? Luxurious? Adventure? Budget-friendly?

🔄 Consistency

Same brand voice, colors, quality across ALL posts. Build recognition over time.

Week 12 Activities

Marketing outputs

📝 Output 1 (Session 1): Digital Marketing Analysis Worksheet
Find 3 real tourism social media posts (different businesses). For each, identify: target audience, value proposition, call-to-action, channel strategy, visual quality. Rate each 1-5.

Output 2 (Session 2): Social Media Content Plan v1
Create a 7-day content plan for your tour package/business:
• Day, content type (photo/video/story/reel), topic, caption draft, hashtags (5-10), posting time
• Include at least 3 content pillars (e.g., destination highlights, behind-the-scenes, customer testimonials, tips, promos)
• Must show variety – not 7 identical "book now" posts

Poster Elements

Tourism poster must-haves

  • Headline: Short, attention-grabbing (5-8 words max)
  • Hero image: Beautiful, high-quality destination photo
  • Offer details: Package name, key inclusions, dates (if seasonal)
  • Price: Clear, prominent (if promotional)
  • CTA: "Book Now," "Message Us," "Limited Slots!"
  • Contact: Phone, Facebook page, or website
  • Branding: Logo or business name visible
Week 13 Outputs

Design outputs

📝 Output 1 (Session 1): Tourism Promotional Poster Draft
Design ONE promotional poster in Canva for your tour package. Must include all 7 elements. Size: 1080x1080 (Instagram post). Download as PNG.

Output 2 (Session 2): Revised Poster + Caption Set
After peer critique, revise your poster based on feedback. Create a caption set: 5 different captions for 5 different post types (promo, behind-the-scenes, testimonial, tips, engagement question). Each caption: 2-4 sentences + 5-10 hashtags.

Evaluation criteria: Visual quality, readability, brand fit, accuracy of info, call-to-action clarity

Both → 04_Marketing folder (PNG + Doc with captions)

Reviews

Responding to reviews

Review TypeResponse ApproachExample Opening
⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ PositiveThank, highlight, invite back"Thank you so much for the wonderful review, [Name]! We're thrilled you enjoyed..."
⭐⭐⭐ NeutralThank, acknowledge concern, offer improvement"Thank you for your feedback, [Name]. We appreciate your honesty and want to address..."
⭐ NegativeApologize, take responsibility, offer resolution, invite offline"We sincerely apologize for your experience, [Name]. This is not the standard we aim for..."

⚠️ Never: Argue publicly, blame the customer, ignore negative reviews, get defensive, or copy-paste the same response to everyone. Each response should feel personal and genuine.
