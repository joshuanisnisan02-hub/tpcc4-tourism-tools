Part 1

File Naming
Conventions

Small habit, big professional impact

Bad vs Good

File naming: Bad vs. Good

❌ Unprofessional

  • Document1.docx
  • final FINAL (2).docx
  • untitled.xlsx
  • IMG_20240315_143022.jpg
  • asdfgh.pdf

✅ Professional

  • Bohol_3D2N_Itinerary_v2.docx
  • TourCosting_Cebu_Island_Hop.xlsx
  • BookingConfirm_Garcia_20240315.pdf
  • Promo_Poster_Summer2024_v1.png
  • SalesReport_March2024.xlsx
Formula

File naming formula

[Type]_[Description]_[Date/Version].[extension]

Examples:
Itinerary_Bohol_3D2N_v1.docx
Costing_Siargao_Package_20240820.xlsx
Email_Draft_Booking_Confirm.docx
Poster_Summer_Promo_FINAL.png
Quiz1_TPCC4_Week1.docx

📏 Rules: No spaces (use underscore _), no special characters (!@#), keep it short but descriptive, use versioning (v1, v2) or dates (YYYYMMDD), be consistent.

Version Control

Why versions matter

In tourism, documents go through multiple revisions. Itineraries change, prices update, marketing materials get feedback. Version control prevents confusion.

Version numbersItinerary_Bohol_v1, v2, v3Ongoing drafts with revisions

Best Practices

Folder best practices

  • Use numbered prefixes (01_, 02_) to control sort order
  • Keep folder depth shallow – max 3 levels deep for easy navigation
  • One file, one home – avoid duplicating files across folders
  • Archive completed work – move old items to an "Archive" subfolder
  • Use consistent naming – same pattern across all folders

Common Mistakes

Email don'ts

❌ No subject

Looks spam-like, hard to find later, unprofessional

❌ ALL CAPS

Reads as shouting, aggressive tone

❌ Text speak

"pls cnfrm ur bkng asap" – not professional

❌ No greeting

Jumping straight to demands feels rude

❌ Reply All abuse

Not everyone needs every reply

❌ Huge attachments

Use Drive links for files over 5MB

Part 4

Shared Drive &
Privacy Awareness

Working with others professionally in digital spaces

Shared Drives

Google Drive sharing rules

  • Viewer: Can see but not edit (for clients reviewing documents)
  • Commenter: Can suggest changes but not directly edit
  • Editor: Full edit access (for team members working on the file)

⚠️ Golden Rule: Always share with the MINIMUM permission needed. Don't give Editor access to everyone. Only share with people who NEED the file.

Privacy

Privacy awareness

  • Customer data is sacred: Names, numbers, emails, passport info – handle with care
  • Don't share screenshots of customer conversations without permission
  • Password hygiene: Strong passwords, don't share, don't reuse
  • Public WiFi caution: Don't access sensitive files on unsecured networks
  • Data Piracy Act (RA 10173): Philippine law on personal data protection
Online Identity

Professional online identity

Do ✅

  • Use professional email (firstname.lastname@)
  • Clean profile photo on work accounts
  • Consistent name across platforms
  • Think before posting publicly
  • Separate personal and professional accounts

Don't ❌

  • Use cutesy emails for work (babyko2002@...)
  • Post inappropriate content on public profiles
  • Badmouth employers/clients online
  • Share company information publicly
  • Use personal accounts for business files
